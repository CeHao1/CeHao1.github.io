Ce Hao (郝策)

Incoming Assistant Professor · Zhongguancun Academy

I am Ce Hao, and I will be joining the Zhongguancun Academy as an Assistant Professor in 2026. My research focuses on bimanual mobile robot manipulation and the dynamics and locomotion of quadrupedal and humanoid robots.

I am currently a PhD candidate in the National University of Singapore, supervised by Prof. Harold Soh . Before NUS, I studied in the University of California, Berkeley as a PhD student in Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka's lab.
